# Your Profile

Access your profile by clicking your **avatar** in the top bar and selecting **"My Profile"**.

![Profile](/img/screenshots/profile-personal-info.png)

### Profile Sidebar

- **Personal Information** — Edit your basic details
- **Change Password** — Update your password
- **Approvals** — Manage Out of Office (approvers only)
- **Two-Factor Authentication** — Enable or disable 2FA
- **Log out** — Sign out

---

## Personal Information

Update your name, email, phone, and address. Click **"Save Profile"** to save, or **"Cancel"** to revert.

To update your avatar, click **"Upload New Photo"**. An existing photo shows a **"Remove"** option.

---

## Changing Your Password

1. Enter your **Current Password**.
2. Enter and confirm your **New Password**.
3. Click **"Change Password"**.

:::warning
If your current password is incorrect, the fields clear and you'll see an error.
:::

## Two-Factor Authentication

![2FA Setup](/img/screenshots/profile-2fa.png)

### Enabling 2FA

1. Toggle the switch to **ON**.
2. **Step 1** — Scan the QR code with your authenticator app (or enter the secret key manually). Click **Next**.
3. **Step 2** — Enter the 6-digit code from your app.
4. **Step 3** — Copy and save your **10 recovery codes** in a secure location. Each code works once.

### Disabling 2FA

Toggle to **OFF** and confirm with your current password and a 6-digit code from your app.

### Enforced 2FA

If your organization enforces 2FA, the toggle is locked ON. The setup modal opens automatically at login and cannot be skipped.

## Out of Office (Approvers)

![Out of Office](/img/screenshots/profile-out-of-office.png)

When active, purchase requests are rerouted to other available approvers.

| State | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Inactive** | Available for approvals |
| **Active** | Currently out of office |
| **Scheduled** | Out of office scheduled for a future date |

### Activate Immediately

Toggle the switch to **ON**.

### Schedule Out of Office

1. Click **"Schedule from a specific date"**.
2. Select the **Active From** date (today or later).
3. Click **Schedule**.

When scheduled, use **Cancel** to cancel or **Change** to pick a different date.

### Deactivate

Toggle to **OFF** and confirm in the dialog.
