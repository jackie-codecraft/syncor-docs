# The Product Catalog

The Product Catalog is where you browse, search, and add products to your purchase requests.

![Product Catalog](/img/screenshots/product-catalog-grid.png)

## Accessing the Catalog

Click **Browse** in the sidebar, or use **Cmd+K** / **Ctrl+K** to search from anywhere.

## Site and Contract Selector

At the top of the catalog, a **Site/Contract selector** shows your current site and contract. If you belong to multiple sites, click the selector to switch. Available products may differ by site and contract.

---

## Navigating Categories

Products are organized into a hierarchical category tree displayed as horizontal tabs.

- **All** — Shows all products across categories
- **Category tabs** (e.g., "Food & Beverage", "Office Equipment & Supplies")

Hover over or click a tab with a chevron (▼) to reveal subcategories. When browsing within a category, breadcrumb navigation appears showing your position in the hierarchy:

> Food & Beverage > Coffee & Tea > Tea Bags

Click any breadcrumb segment to navigate back to that level.
