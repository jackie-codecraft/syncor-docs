# Product Detail & Adding to a Request

## Product Detail Page

Click any product card to view its full details.

![Product Detail](/img/screenshots/product-detail.png)

The detail page shows the product image (browseable if multiple), name, category, price, SKU, and unit of measure.

### Variant Selector

If the product has variants (sizes, colors), a selector appears. Choosing a variant updates the SKU and pricing.

### Expandable Sections

| Section | Default | Contents |
|---------|---------|----------|
| **Description** | Expanded | Detailed product description |
| **Specifications** | Collapsed | Attribute table (material, weight, dimensions) |
| **Attachments** | Collapsed | Downloadable files (spec sheets, safety data) |

### Related Products

A **Related Products** section may appear below the details if enabled by your organization.

---

## Adding Products to a Request

### From the Catalog Grid

1. Set the desired quantity using the +/- buttons.
2. Click **Add**.
3. The cart icon updates to show the new item count.

### From the Product Detail Page

1. Set the desired quantity using the +/- buttons.
2. Click **Add to Request**.

:::note
If you don't have an active draft purchase request, one will be created automatically. If your organization has multiple sites, you may be prompted to select a site first.
:::

:::tip
You can keep browsing and adding products — all items accumulate in your current draft until you're ready to submit.
:::
