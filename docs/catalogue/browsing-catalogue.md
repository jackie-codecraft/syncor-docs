# Browsing the Catalogue

The Syncor catalogue is your organisation's curated list of pre-approved products and services available for purchase. Items in the catalogue have been vetted by your procurement team and come with pre-negotiated pricing, saving you time and ensuring compliance with your supplier agreements.

## Accessing the Catalogue

You can browse the catalogue in two ways:

- **During a purchase request** — click **Add Items** while creating a new request. The catalogue browser opens in a side panel.
- **Standalone browsing** — click **Catalogue** in the left navigation menu. This lets you explore without committing to a request.

## Catalogue Layout

The catalogue is organised into **categories** (e.g. Office Supplies, IT Equipment, Facilities, Professional Services). Each category contains **sub-categories** and individual **product listings**.

Each product listing displays:

- **Product name and description**
- **Supplier name**
- **Unit price** (excluding or including VAT, as configured)
- **Unit of measure** (e.g. each, box of 10, per day)
- **Lead time** — estimated delivery or fulfilment time
- **Stock status** (where applicable)

## Adding Items to a Request

1. Find the product you want (see [Search and Filter](./search-and-filter) for tips).
2. Review the product details by clicking the listing.
3. Enter the **quantity** you need.
4. Click **Add to Request**. The item appears in your request's Order Summary.
5. Continue browsing to add more items, or close the catalogue panel to return to your request.

:::note
If you need something that isn't in the catalogue, use the **Free-text Request** option when creating your purchase request. This allows you to describe the item and enter an estimated cost. Free-text requests may require additional approval steps.
:::

## Catalogue Availability

Your view of the catalogue depends on your role and department. Some items or categories may be restricted to specific teams or roles (e.g. IT hardware may only be orderable by the IT department). If you believe an item should be available to you but isn't visible, contact your Procurement Manager.
