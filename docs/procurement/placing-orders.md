# Placing a Purchase Order

A **Purchase Request** (PR) in Syncor is the formal record of your intent to buy goods or services. Once raised, it moves through your organisation's approval chain before becoming a confirmed Purchase Order (PO) sent to the supplier.

## Before You Begin

Make sure you have:

- A valid Syncor account with the **Requester** role
- The cost centre or project code to charge the purchase to
- A general idea of what you need (you'll select items from the catalogue)

## Raising a Purchase Request

1. From the **Dashboard**, click **New Request** in the top navigation bar.
2. Enter a **Request Title** that clearly describes what you're ordering (e.g. *"Office chairs — Design team Q2"*).
3. Select your **Department** and **Cost Centre** from the dropdowns.
4. (Optional) Enter a **Delivery Date** if the goods or services are needed by a specific date.
5. Click **Add Items** to open the product catalogue browser.
6. Search or browse for each item, set the **quantity**, and click **Add to Request**. Repeat for all items.
7. Review the line items and total in the **Order Summary** panel on the right.
8. Add any **notes or justification** in the Notes field — this is visible to approvers.
9. Attach any supporting documents (quotes, specifications) using the **Attachments** section.
10. Click **Submit for Approval**.

Once submitted, you will receive an email confirmation and the request will appear in your **My Requests** list with status *Pending Approval*.

## Tracking Your Request

Navigate to **My Requests** to see the status of all your purchase requests:

- **Draft** — saved but not yet submitted
- **Pending Approval** — awaiting review by one or more approvers
- **Approved** — all approvals complete; a PO is being generated
- **Rejected** — declined by an approver (see the request for the reason)
- **Ordered** — PO has been sent to the supplier
- **Received** — goods/services have been marked as received

## Editing or Withdrawing a Request

You can edit a request while it is in **Draft** status. Once submitted, you cannot edit it — contact your approver or raise a new request if changes are needed. To withdraw a pending request, open it and click **Withdraw**, which returns it to Draft status.
