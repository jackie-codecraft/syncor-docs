# Your Dashboard

The Dashboard is the first page you see after logging in. It provides a personalized overview of your procurement activity.

![Dashboard](/img/screenshots/dashboard.png)

The dashboard is widget-based — different widgets appear depending on your role:

- **Quick Actions** — Shortcut cards for common tasks
- **Approval Metrics** — Statistics for approvers
- **Pending Requests** — PRs awaiting approval (approvers only)
- **Recent Activity** — Timeline of recent procurement events
- **Help and Resources** — Links to support resources

:::note
If no widgets are configured for your account, you'll see a "Widgets not available" message. Contact your administrator.
:::

---

## Quick Actions

**For Requesters:**
- **New Purchase Request** — Start creating a new purchase request
- **My Purchase Requests** — View and manage your requisitions
- **My Draft Purchase Requests** — Continue working on saved drafts
- **Sourcing Requests** — Create or view sourcing requests

**For Approvers:**
- **Pending Requests** — Jump to PRs awaiting your approval (shows count)
- **Approval History** — View your past approval actions

---

## Approval Metrics (Approvers)

| Metric | Color | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| **Pending Approvals** | Yellow | PRs waiting for your approval |
| **Approved** | Green | PRs you've approved |
| **Rejected** | Red | PRs you've rejected |
| **Total** | Brand color | Total PRs through your queue |

Filter by time period: **All Time**, **Today**, **Week**, or **Month**.

---

## Pending Requests (Approvers)

Shows up to 5 pending requisitions in a compact table with quick-action controls. Click **"View All"** to navigate to the full Approvals page.

---

## Recent Activity & Support

### Recent Activity

A chronological feed of procurement events: submissions, approvals, rejections, order confirmations, shipments, and deliveries. Click any item to navigate to that requisition's detail page.

:::tip
If a request has been rejected, you'll see a **"Revise & Resubmit"** link on the activity item.
:::
