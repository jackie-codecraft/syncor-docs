# Navigating the Interface

![Dashboard Overview](/img/screenshots/dashboard.png)

## Sidebar Navigation

The left sidebar is your primary navigation:

- **Dashboard** — Your home page with quick actions and activity feed
- **Browse** — The product catalog
- **Request** — Your purchase requests
- **Approve** — Approval queue (approvers only)
- **Source** — Sourcing requests
- **Settings** and **Support** at the bottom

Collapse the sidebar to icons by clicking the **Toggle Sidebar** (hamburger) button at the top. Your preference is remembered across sessions.

## Top Bar

From left to right:

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Search Bar** | Search for products, orders, or actions. Shortcut: **Cmd+K** (Mac) / **Ctrl+K** (Windows) |
| **Language Selector** | Switch between available languages |
| **Cart Icon** | Shows items in your current draft. Displays item count badge. |
| **Notifications Bell** | Shows unread count ("99+" if over 99). Click to open the dropdown. |
| **Profile Avatar** | Click for your name, **My Profile** link, and **Logout** |

:::tip
The cart icon only appears if you have permission to create purchase requests and have an active draft.
:::
