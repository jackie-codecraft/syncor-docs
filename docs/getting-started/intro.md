# Introduction to Syncor

Syncor is a cloud-based B2B procurement portal designed to streamline the way organisations source, approve, and track purchases. Whether you're a department head raising a purchase request or a finance manager reviewing spend, Syncor gives your team a single, auditable place to manage procurement from request to delivery.

## What Syncor Does

Syncor connects requesters, approvers, and suppliers in one workflow, eliminating email chains, spreadsheet trackers, and offline paper trails. Key capabilities include:

- **Structured purchase requests** — raise orders against a defined product catalogue with per-line budget codes
- **Multi-tier approval routing** — configurable approval chains based on spend threshold, department, or supplier
- **Supplier catalogue management** — import and maintain approved supplier catalogues with live pricing
- **Real-time spend visibility** — dashboard and reports showing committed spend vs. budget at team and company level
- **Audit trail** — every action (create, approve, reject, modify) is logged with timestamp and user

## Who Uses Syncor?

| Role | What they do in Syncor |
|---|---|
| **Requester** | Browse the catalogue, raise purchase requests, track order status |
| **Approver** | Review and approve or reject purchase requests within their delegated authority |
| **Procurement Manager** | Manage the supplier catalogue, configure approval rules, generate reports |
| **Administrator** | Manage users, roles, and system-wide settings |
